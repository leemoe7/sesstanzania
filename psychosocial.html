PSYCHOSOCIAL COMMUNITY CAPACITY BUILDING

The communities we are working to serve are hampered by traditional and taboos yielding to some gender abused hence calling for social rehabilitations. Therefore the organization always advocates and seeks to see all member communities living in harmony without oppression of any other side of gender. In doing that, the organization joins efforts with other development stakeholders to fight the web of gender based violence’s issues harmoniously.

• Advocate for anti-gender based violence’s issues.

• Promote equity and inclusions issues (serving people without geographical discriminations).

• Being neutral organization (serving communities and/or people regardless of religious altitude).
